Use Factory Reset to restore the device to its original factory configuration.

## How to Use

- Open the Actions menu for the device.
- Select Factory Reset Device.
- Select Keep Redirector Bottom to persist device certificates after the reset(Optional).
- When prompted with the device serial number, click Start Reset to confirm.


![Factory reset confirmation](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image47.webp)

## Result

- The device will reset to its factory settings.
- All existing configurations on the device will be removed.
