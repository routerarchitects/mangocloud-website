Use Wi-Fi Scan to detect nearby wireless networks from the device.

## How to Use

- Click the **Wi-Fi Scan** button to initiate the process.
- In the popup window, click **Scan** to start scanning.
- You can download the Wi-Fi scan results after the scan completes.

![Wi-Fi scan command](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image20.webp)

![Wi-Fi scan results](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image52.webp)

## Result

- The device scans and returns a list of nearby wireless networks.
- The results include details such as SSID, signal strength, and other relevant network information.
