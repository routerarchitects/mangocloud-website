title: Creating Venues
---

Steps:

1. Select the target Entity you created.
2. Go to Venues (tab/section).
3. Click + Venue (top-right).

![Add Venue button](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image50.webp)

4. Enter Venue name (optional description) and click Save.

![Venue details form](/img/operations/mango-cloud-operations-guide/image49.webp)

Result: The Venue is created under the Entity, and you can assign devices to it for provisioning.
