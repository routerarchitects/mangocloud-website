# About MangoCloud

MangoCloud is an open-source cloud controller for OpenWiFi Access Points, Switches, and OpenLAN Gateways. Router Architects launched the project to offer a production-grade alternative to proprietary controllers while keeping deployment flexibility for ISPs and operators.

## Mission

- Provide scalable automation for massive OpenWiFi fleets
- Lower operational costs through open tooling and orchestration
- Foster a vendor-neutral ecosystem backed by a Contributor License Agreement

## Maintainers

- **Router Architects** — Core maintainers driving roadmap alignment, releases, and community governance.
- **Contributors** — ISPs, hardware vendors, and community members delivering features, documentation, and validation.

## Governance

- Public roadmap and issue tracker
- CLA-backed contributions to protect community IP
- Transparent release notes and upgrade guidance
